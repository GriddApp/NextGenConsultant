# Feature to market speed up track

Hypothesis: weekly deployment instead monthly is possible with upholding same or better quality.

## Research questions and flow

Benchmark against similarly mature competitors. Are we in the bottom, middle or high brackets?

What levers can we pull to speed up the deployment cycle without compromising quality and customer satisfaction?

How does an ideal tech organization looks like?

What areas our client lags behind and what are the steps needed to close the gap?

What are the dependencies that create 4 week deployment instead of any-time deployment?

## Ideal tech organization

- Clear and well-communicated Tech North Star -> this speeds up decision bottlenecks by increasing alignment

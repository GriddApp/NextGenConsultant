# Our Way of Working

## Team roles

- **1x Engagement Manager**: managing client counterpart to enable their leading of the project, fill in the storyline and improve where needed.
- **2x Analyst**, leading upskilling and customer satisfaction tracks.
- **1x Principal Architect**: leading feature to market track.
- **Associate Partner**: managing sponsor relationships, unlock necessary resources on client side, delivery risk owner.
- **Responsible Partner**: managing client relationship, escalate up top, owner of the final storyline delivered to the client.

## Cadence

Project:
- **[week 0] Kick-off**: client context, goals and the tracks we are to explore during the engagement.
- **[week 0] Team learning**: each team member is familier with the working norms, we set our team norms and we get to understand each member's development goals.
- **[12 weeks] Engagement**: weekly and monthly cadence detailed below.
- **[week 12] Hand-**over**: after final steerco, we wrap up by creating the hand-over docs and record in both client and Firm secure systems.

> _**Warning**_: this project repository contains all engagement document, including non-client facing, non-shareable information such as our team's internal development goals! Take extra care to clean sensitive info out before hand-over but file the entire repository in the Firm's side as is!

## Team norms

Daily standups at 9:30am, client-time. Format: achieved yesterday - to be achieved today - impediments stopping me. PS topics separately with identified participants.

No meetings scheduled after 6pm.

2-3 days before steerco may be stretched, after steerco solving 2-3 hours timeboxed immediate items then free time for team.

Weekly 2 hours of PS with partner. Always align messaging with sponsor before demos and steercos.

Monthly team dinner. Monthly client-team dinner.

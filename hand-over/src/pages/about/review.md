# Feedback & Review Guide

This site includes a built-in review tool so you can annotate any page and share your feedback with the consultant team — no account, no installation, and no external service required.

---

## The two buttons

In the bottom-right corner of every page you will find two small buttons:

| Button | What it does |
|---|---|
| **⬇ (green)** | Downloads the current page as a fully self-contained HTML file |
| **📌 (dark)** | Opens the sticker panel for placing and managing review annotations |

---

## How to give feedback

### Step 1 — Request a page export

Ask your consultant to send you the `.html` export of the page you want to review. It is a single file — no Docusaurus installation or internet connection required to open it.

### Step 2 — Open and annotate

1. Save the `.html` file anywhere on your computer and open it in a browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari).
2. Click the **📌 button** in the bottom-right corner.
3. Enter your name or initials in the **Reviewer** field.
4. Pick a color to categorise your comments (labels are shown in the panel).
5. Click **Start Reviewing** — the cursor changes to a crosshair.
6. Click anywhere on the page to drop a sticky note and type your comment.
7. Repeat for every piece of feedback, switching colors as needed.
8. Click **Stop Reviewing** when you are done.

### Step 3 — Export and send your annotations

In the 📌 panel, click **Export YAML**.

A file called `<page-title>.review.yaml` is downloaded. Send it back to the consultant team by email, Slack, or any other channel you already use.

That's it — no upload, no account, nothing leaves your hands except the single YAML file.

---

## How the consultant team uses your feedback

The team imports your `.review.yaml` file directly into the live site. Your sticky notes appear anchored to the exact elements you annotated. If several reviewers send files, the consultant imports them one at a time; stickers from different reviewers are merged automatically, so none of your comments are lost.

---

## Privacy & security

- **Nothing is uploaded anywhere.** The exported HTML file and the YAML annotation file travel only between you and the consultant team, through whatever channel you choose.
- **No account required.** There is no login, no tracking, and no third-party service involved.
- **Offline-ready.** The exported HTML file works without an internet connection — all styles, fonts, and images are embedded inside it.

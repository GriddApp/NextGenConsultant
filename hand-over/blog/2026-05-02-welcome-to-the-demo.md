This site is a small demo of what consulting hand-overs can look like when they stop being PowerPoint files and start being an agent-ready repository.

{/* truncate */}

The structure is super simple: three folders — context, research, synthesis — feeding into this Docusaurus site.

Mermaid diagrams, Recharts charts and Gridd embeds all live next to the markdown they explain, and every claim points back to the underlying data.

Have a look around: open the engagement, follow a track, click through to the raw research.

Feedback, as always, welcome.

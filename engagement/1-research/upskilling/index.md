### Notes on the benchmarks

- **Software Engineer**: Western EU senior averages cluster around €100k
  (UK €126k, DE €112k, NL €110k, FR €96k, ES €83k). Staff reaches €110–140k
  at scale-ups; Principal €130–180k+ at top tier.
- **Product Manager**: EMEA-wide average for PMs sits around €120k, but
  median country anchors are lower — DE €67k mid / €88k senior, Berlin €82k /
  €86k. UK trajectory £39k junior to £82k senior.
- **UX Designer**: Junior ~€32k EU-wide, mid-level ~€48k, senior ~€86k. DE
  entry €52k, senior 8+y €85k. Lead/Principal stretches into €100–140k at
  top firms.
- **Data Scientist**: typically €10–20k above analyst at the same level.
  DE entry €79k, senior 8+y €140k; NL senior €90k; UK senior £87k.

# Tech North Star

The ideal aspiration for the group-wide tech organizations supporting all business goals.

## Architectural Pillars

Four main pillars underpin the successful and lightning-fast digital delivery:

1. **Evolutionary architecture**: heterogeneous tech stack, using the proper tool for any given job, not sticking to a single stack just for comfort.
2. **Testing trophy**: focus on end-to-end testing instead of nuclear unit testing as 80% of bugs come from systems interfacing (Pareto-principle applied to problem prioritization).
3. **Proactive maintenance**: important that we as the organization and team are ahead of our users and customers at discovering issues and ideally even fixing them before serious consequences appear.
4. **Developer enablement**: balancing feature focus with organization and individual healthy and fulfilling work environment is the ultimate unlock for long term success.

All four are equally important for the organizational success. Forget one or just deprioritize long enough against any other and the outcome will degrade sooner or later.
